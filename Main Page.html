Introduction to Cybersecurity
By: Asher, Edwin, Grandy, Zi Yuan, Rainer and Xavier



Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting anything online from unauthorized access, attacks, or damage. As our world becomes increasingly connected through the internet and digital technologies, cybersecurity plays a critical role in protecting our personal information, business operations, and national security. It involves a wide range of tools, strategies and well-knowledged manpower such as firewalls, encryption, security protocols and trained personnels to detect threats, prevent breaches, and respond to cyber incidents. With the growing threat of cybercrime, from hacking and identity theft to ransomware and data breaches, cybersecurity is essential for ensuring trust, privacy, and safety in the digital age
